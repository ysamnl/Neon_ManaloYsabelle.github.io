NEVER AGAIN. DO NOT FORGET.

It's up to us.

The rest is up to us. We must be able to discern credible articles from fake articles. We Filipinos must work hard together to achieve the best for our country. We must always remember that our leaders, presidents and etc. should not be feared by its people. It is us, the Filipinos who fund them and give them power. Let us remember history and the things they taught us.
